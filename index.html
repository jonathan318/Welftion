ABOUT US

Introduction :

Since 1999 M.A.majid science college (MAMSC), EIIN: 135344, Institution Code: 2178, Upazilla:kapasia-1730, District:Gazipur,Dhaka,Bngladeh. has been working in providing skills training for unemployed youth on different trades such as Computer, Electrical, Driving, Mobile Servicing, Tailoring, Engine mechanics and welding to reduce unemployment and poverty . 3000 unemployed youth received skills training from MAIT , Dhaka on different trades and 300 are still receiving training. More than 1000 graduate got employment opportunities since 2007. As a part of sustainability of MAIT, , Dhaka has got the registration from Bangladesh Technical Education Board (BTEB) for 4 years Diploma-in-Engineering courses especially for 3 subjects such as Diploma in Computer, Electrical & Civil Engineering. We are also affiliated by Bangladesh road transport authority (BRTA) to run motor driving school.

ADMISSION REQUIREMENT

Diploma-in-Engineering admission:Diploma-in-Engineering course admission starts at all Government/equivalent polytechnic institution under Bangladesh Technical Education Board (BTEB). The last date of application is 12 June 2013. Students who passed SSC in 2011, 2012 & 2013 with GPA 3.00 in Mathematics/Higher Mathematics and have total GPA 3.50 are eligible to apply. And students who passed 2 year Trade Course approved by BTEB are also eligible to apply. For more information about the admission please
